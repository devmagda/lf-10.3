Welcome to Our Cleaning Events Platform

At CleanCity Together, we bring communities together to make our cities sparkle! Whether you're here to organize an event, join as a volunteer, or just curious about how we help the environment, we're thrilled to have you.

What We Do

We organize cleaning events all over the city, helping reduce waste, beautify public spaces, and create lasting connections between neighbors. Together, we make a difference, one event at a time.

Why Join Us?

  • Make a Positive Impact: Every piece of litter picked up helps protect our environment.
  • Meet New People: Our events bring together people from all walks of life to work toward a common goal.
  • Have Fun: Cleaning might sound like a chore, but we make it enjoyable with music, snacks, and a community vibe!

How It Works

  1. Join an Event: Browse upcoming cleaning events and sign up to join the fun.
  2. Create an Event: Organize a cleaning event in your neighborhood and rally volunteers to help out.
  3. Make a Difference: After the event, see the immediate impact of your efforts and celebrate with new friends!
